🛠️ Three Ways to Search & Add Images

Choose the method that fits your workflow

1 📚 Media Library

Access the "All Sources Images" tab directly from Media > Add New or any media picker. Search across multiple banks simultaneously and download images directly to your library.

2 🧱 Gutenberg Block

In the Block Editor, add the "ASI Images" block. Browse and search images from all configured sources, then insert directly into your content with one click.

3 🎨 Elementor Widget

Drag and drop the "ASI Image" widget in Elementor. Click "Choose Image" to open the explorer and search from any configured source—perfect for page builders!
